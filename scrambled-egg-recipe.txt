Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - 3 tablespoons of olive oil
 - 1 teaspoon of curry powder
 - 2 teaspoons of pepper
 - 1 pinch of salt

Directions:
  1. In a medium frying pan, add 3 tablespoons of olive oil and heat with low heat.
  2. Break the eggs and pour the yoke and egg whit into a bowl.
  3. Sprinkle the pinch of salt into the bowl of egg and wisk until the yoke and white are mixed smoothly.
  4. Increase the heat on the frying pan and add the wisked egg into the pan.
  5. Sprinkle the pepper and curry powder into the pan while you stir.
  6. Lower the heat and keep stiring for about 30 seconds.
  7. After stiring put off the heat. Your scrambled eggs are ready.
